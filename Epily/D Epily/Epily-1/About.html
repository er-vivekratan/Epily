About US

At Epily, we are passionate about improving the lives of those affected by epilepsy through innovative prediction technology and compassionate support. Our mission is to empower individuals with epilepsy by providing accurate, timely predictions that help manage and mitigate the impact of seizures.

Our Mission

Our goal is to offer a reliable tool that not only predicts seizures but also enhances the quality of life for our users. By leveraging advanced algorithms and machine learning, we strive to provide peace of mind and a sense of control to those living with epilepsy.

Our Story

Epily was founded by a team of dedicated professionals from diverse backgrounds in healthcare, technology, and data science. Inspired by personal experiences and a shared commitment to making a difference, we embarked on a journey to develop a tool that would transform epilepsy management.

Our Team

We are a cohesive group of experts in neurology, software development, and artificial intelligence. Our interdisciplinary approach ensures that we remain at the forefront of innovation, constantly refining our technology to better serve our users. We believe in the power of collaboration and are committed to creating a supportive, inclusive community
